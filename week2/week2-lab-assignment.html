üè¢ Corporate Insider Threat Investigation

Date: Thursday, February 6, 2026

Your Role: Digital Forensics Investigator, Badge #DF-3142

Case Number: 2026-CORP-0215

You are a digital forensics investigator for Redwood Cybersecurity Solutions. You have been called to NorthCoast Manufacturing, a company that produces proprietary industrial control systems for the timber industry.

Background:

NorthCoast Manufacturing's Chief Technology Officer, Rebecca Martinez (employee ID: NCM-CTO-001), contacted your firm yesterday evening (February 5, 2026 at 18:30) reporting a suspected intellectual property theft.

The suspect is David Chen (employee ID: NCM-ENG-0847), a senior software engineer who resigned abruptly on February 4, 2026. Chen gave no notice and did not return company equipment. HR reported that Chen accepted a position with a direct competitor, TimberTech Industries.

On February 5, the IT department discovered that Chen's company laptop and two external storage devices were still in his locked office. Security camera footage shows Chen accessing his office on February 3, 2026 (his last day in the building) and working at his computer from 19:45 to 22:30 (after normal business hours).

Your Assignment:

You arrive at NorthCoast Manufacturing headquarters (4567 Industrial Parkway, Ukiah, CA 95482) at 09:00 on February 6, 2026. Rebecca Martinez meets you in the main lobby and escorts you to Chen's former office (Building B, 3rd Floor, Office 312).

Martinez provides you with authorization to collect all digital devices from Chen's office for forensic examination. She will serve as witness to the evidence collection.

üìù Your Task

‚úÖ Requirements

Complete THREE separate chain of custody forms (one for each evidence item) using the information provided in the scenario. Each form must include:

  1. Complete Evidence Description
    • Case number and item number (format: 2026-CORP-0215-001, 002, 003)
    • Make, model, and serial number
    • Physical description and condition
    • Location where found
  2. Collection Information
    • Your name and badge number
    • Date and time collected (use 24-hour format)
    • Specific location (building, floor, office number)
    • Packaging method and evidence bag number
  3. Witness Information
    • Witness name: Rebecca Martinez
    • Witness title/ID: CTO, employee ID NCM-CTO-001
  4. Chain of Custody Record
    • Initial custody: You (investigator)
    • Transfer to: James Wilson, Evidence Custodian (RCS-EC-001)
    • Date/time of transfer: 2026-02-06 at 11:50
    • Purpose: Secure storage in evidence locker
    • Final location: Evidence Locker #15-C
