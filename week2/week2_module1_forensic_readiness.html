3. Eight Key Components

Laws to Know:

  • ECPA: Electronic Communications Privacy Act
  • CFAA: Computer Fraud and Abuse Act
  • State Laws: Vary by jurisdiction

Regulations:

  • HIPAA: 60-day breach notification
  • PCI-DSS: Mandatory forensic investigation
  • GDPR: 72-hour notification, ‚Ç¨20M fines
  • SOX: Evidence retention requirements

System Logs: 6-12 months (industry standard)

Email: 1-7 years (high forensic value, high privacy concern)

Access Logs: 90 days - 1 year

Key Principle: Balance forensic value against storage costs and privacy

Critical Log Sources:

  • üîê Authentication events
  • üìÑ File access and modifications
  • üåê Network traffic
  • ‚öôÔ∏è System changes
  • üëë Privileged actions

‚ö†Ô∏è Critical: NTP (Network Time Protocol) for synchronized timestamps

Forensics integrates throughout IR lifecycle:

  • Preparation: Tools ready, staff trained
  • Detection: Volatile data collection
  • Containment: Evidence preservation
  • Recovery: Integrity verification
  • Post-Incident: Comprehensive analysis

Chain of Custody:

  • Who: Names and badge numbers
  • What: Description and serial numbers
  • When: Date and time
  • Where: Location details
  • Why: Purpose of collection
  • How: Methods and tools used

‚ö†Ô∏è Warning: Chain of custody breaks are case-killers

Essential Tools:

  • Imaging: FTK Imager, dd, Guymager
  • Analysis: Autopsy, EnCase, X-Ways
  • Memory: Volatility, Rekall
  • Network: Wireshark, NetworkMiner
  • Mobile: Cellebrite, Oxygen
  • Write Blockers: Tableau, WiebeTech

üí° Use NIST-validated tools for court defensibility

General Staff: Annual awareness (recognize incidents)

IT/Security: 2-3 day IR course (first responders)

Specialists: 40+ hours annually + certifications

  • Entry: EC-Council CHFI
  • Intermediate: GIAC GCFE
  • Advanced: GIAC GCFA
  • Law Enforcement: IACIS CFCE
  • Legal Counsel: Cybersecurity specialized attorneys
  • Consultants: Forensic experts for complex cases
  • Law Enforcement: FBI, Secret Service contacts
  • Industry Groups: ISACs, InfraGard, HTCIA
